#Fueling and Staring Engines.

Expected time in the clock is between 5:00 - 6:00 AM. The more Early the more Better

Getting Enough Sleep

Sleeping for at least 6-8 hours. No more no less. One of the behaviours this can be accomplished is going to bed on time with a sense of gratitude in mind. A sense of satisfaction and fulfillness. Peaceful sleep and a peaceful mind will supercharge the body.

#Taking Off - I am Recharged and I am set off for the Day

Expected time in the clock is between 8:30 - 9:30. The more Early the more Better

Planning (1 hour)

  • Maintain Todos for the work sessions. (3 Sessions - 2 intense 1 light)
  • Each intense session is up of 3 hours and light is of 1.5 hours.
  • Schedule Meetings into Light sessions and main task into Intense
  • Clean the Desk and plan the calander.
  • Check Phone for maximun 10 mins. No disctractions ahead.

Intense Session - Ist (3 hours)

  • It consists of 3 hour continious session with breaks only for coffee, water or bathroom.
  • Focus complete on your target to achieve and no more disctractions.
  • Phone to be switched off and If not silent.

Lunch and Walk (1 hour)

  • Enjoy lunch with bare disctraction and Focus on food what you eat.
  • Enjoy the time eating and dont watch anything while eating just focus on the food.
  • If possible walk around 50 -100 steps in room or on stairs. helps the food to settle down.
  • Call friends/ Social media 10 mins. No disctractions Ahead.

Intense Session - 2nd (3 hours)

  • It consists of 3 hour continious session with breaks only for coffee, water or bathroom.
  • Focus complete on your target to achieve and no more disctractions.
  • Phone to be switched off and If not silent.

Evening Tea (1 hour)

  • Socialize with Family and Have some snacks.
  • Talk about things and share gratitude among parents
  • Be humble and respect the moment. Its gold.
  • If possible try thanking them for the life they gave you.
  • In last 20 mins Call friends/ Social media. No disctractions Ahead.

Light Session (1 hour)

  • Wrapping up the Intense and slowing down the ress map.
  • Send mails and clear the table.
  • Try not to leave things on the pending list.
  • Do not regret about the day whether you accomplished the goal or not.
  • Its the fact that you tried, you will succeed sooner or later.
